Guessing Game


Welcome to our Guessing Game on canvas!


Instructions

1. The Guessing Game on Canvas consists of 10 questions related to trivia or drawings on canvas. Your goal is to guess the correct answer for each question!


2. There are 3 levels in the game: Level 1 (Easy), Level 2 (Challenging), and Level 3 (Hard).


- Level 1: This level has 3 trivia questions worth 50 points each (150pts total). No hints are available for this level.


- Level 2: This level has 5 questions about drawings on canvas. Each question is worth 100 points (500pts total). They have hints to help you guess what is being drawn.


- Level 4: This level has only 2 questions and each of them is worth 175 points (300pts total). Only one question has a hint at this level.


3. How we calculate points: You have 3 chances to guess the correct answer for each question. The correct answer on:


- First try: you get 100% of points for that question


- Second try: you get 50% of points for that question


- Third try: you get 33% of points for that question


4. Hints: be careful, every time you choose to see a hint you will lose 10% of the remaing points for that question.


Example: Question 5 is in Level 2 and it is worth 100pts. If you get the correct answer on your first try you should get 100% of points (100pts). But if you choose to see one hint you will lose 10% of 100pts which is equal to 90pts. If you choose to see two hints and you guessed the correct answer on your second try (50% of 100pts = 50pts) you will lose 20% of 50pts which is equal to 40pts.
